About Us

Brainy Codelabs is a coding academy that guides people from all backgrounds to change their lives through career focused education. We shape passionate coders with immersive training to meet industry needs and improve diversity in tech.

We inspire students through innovative curricular and personalized training, focused on their natural ability to achieve and learn. Our hands-on, project focused format fosters a productive learning experience readying them for a life-time of problem solving and innovation.

Our educational approach does not focus on teaching our students a particular language or technology but instead on how to think as a software engineer, with the ability to teach themselves any technlogy of their own. In fact, we believe the majority of the bootcap participants would go on to work with other languages and frameworks than the ones we would teach in class.
